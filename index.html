About Us

Azteca Tae Kwon Do Foundation is dedicated to discipline, leadership, culture, and excellence in Taekwondo.

Founded by Grand Master German López, the school has trained generations of students and competitors through traditional martial arts values and modern competitive training.

Our programs focus on building confidence, respect, self-control, teamwork, and physical conditioning for students of all ages and experience levels.

At Azteca Tae Kwon Do Foundation, students learn more than martial arts. Our school promotes respect, perseverance, discipline, confidence, and leadership both inside and outside the dojang.

We welcome students of all ages and experience levels, creating a supportive environment where beginners and advanced athletes can continue to grow together.

Through traditional training, competition sparring, conditioning, and teamwork, students build both physical strength and mental focus.

Azteca TKD continues to inspire future generations through dedication, hard work, and a passion for excellence in Taekwondo.
